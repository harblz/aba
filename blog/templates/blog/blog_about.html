About This Blog


I made this site, primarily, to help folks who work with behavior analysts (or folks trying to become behavior analysts) learn as much as they can about the science of behavior analysis. To be fully honest, I made this site as a way to practice DJANGO / Python and back + front end web site development, and since I also happen to be a behavior analyst (BCaBA), I figured I could kill two birds as it were by making RBT practice quizzes for new therapists at my clinic so they can practice before sitting for the exam. It's a fun intersection of two of my favorite hobbies!


Check back every once in awhile for updates, and leave a comment below if you wanna let me know a little about yourself and what you think of the site.
