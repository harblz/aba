ABA Practice Quizzes


This site is dedicated to providing free, useful practice quizzes for folks looking forward to taking the big ABA exam(s)! Whether you're sitting for the RBT exam, trying to brush up on terminology and basic principles, or you're hunkering down and preparing for the big BCBA test, I'm doing my best to gather some useful practice materials for ya here!

Check back regularly as I try to add a new set of quiz questions every Sunday!

New Introducing Quiz Forms

Forms are unique versions of quizzes - I will try to make at least 3 "forms" for each concept area so you can practice without doing the same old questions over and over again. If you are feeling solid with form A, take a shot at form B!

Anyways, good luck and happy studying!

RBT Test Structure: What's On The Exam!
Click to see details on the structure & content of the exam

The RBT exam is 2 hours long, and there will be 75 total questions.

Structure & Content of the RBT Exam. Source: BACB Website. Last Updated 7.15.18
** Please note, your exam may include "Trial questions" which are questions the BACB is evaluating for future tests - missing these questions won't count against you. They are not marked as trial questions on the actual exam.
Content Area # of Questions on the BACB RBT Exam
A. Measurement 10
B. Assessment 8
C. Skill Acquisition 24
D. Behavior Reduction 12
E. Documentation and Reporting 10
F. Professional Conduct and Scope of Practice 11
Total Number of Questions 75 (+10 trial** questions)
