Below are vocabulary building exercises - flashcards - aligned with the RBT, BCaBA, and BCBA task list. Try to practice them until you can run a deck completely and effortlessly - and then move on to the next deck! When you finish that, make sure to try the timed fluency versions of each deck. Good luck, and happy studying! Check back regularly as I try to add a new set of quiz questions every Sunday!

The RBT exam is 2 hours long, and there will be 75 total questions.

Structure & Content of the RBT Exam. Source: BACB Website. Last Updated 7.15.18
** Please note, your exam may include "Trial questions" which are questions the BACB is evaluating for future tests - missing these questions won't count against you. They are not marked as trial questions on the actual exam.
